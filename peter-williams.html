I am a an experienced and passionate software craftsman, architect and leader. I love building software the meets the immediate needs of the customers while simultaneously furthering longer term business and technical strategies. This requires listening to and understanding those stakeholders and then building a shared vision about where the product is headed, both feature-wise and architecturally.

Building such a shared vision is something i1 thrive on, but i know that the key to achieving such visions is breaking it down to a series of incremental improvements that can be executed in a sustainable way. Continuous improvement is the way i like to work, both in the technical and process arenas.
